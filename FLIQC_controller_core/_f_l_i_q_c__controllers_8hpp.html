enum FLIQC_controller_core::FLIQC_quad_cost_type

The configuration for the FLIQC controller quadratic cost.

Enumerator
FLIQC_QUAD_COST_IDENTITY 

The cost is the identity matrix \(\frac{1}{2} \boldsymbol{\dot{q}}^\top \boldsymbol{\dot{q}} \).

FLIQC_QUAD_COST_JOINT_VELOCITY_ERROR 

The cost is the joint velocity error \(\frac{1}{2} (\boldsymbol{\dot{q}} - \boldsymbol{\dot{q}}_{\text{guide}})^\top (\boldsymbol{\dot{q}} - \boldsymbol{\dot{q}}_{\text{guide}})\).

FLIQC_QUAD_COST_MASS_MATRIX 

The cost is the mass matrix \(\frac{1}{2} \boldsymbol{\dot{q}}^\top \boldsymbol{M} \boldsymbol{\dot{q}}\).

FLIQC_QUAD_COST_MASS_MATRIX_VELOCITY_ERROR 

The cost is the error weighted in the mass matrix \(\frac{1}{2} (\boldsymbol{\dot{q}} - \boldsymbol{\dot{q}}_{\text{guide}})^\top \boldsymbol{M} (\boldsymbol{\dot{q}} - \boldsymbol{\dot{q}}_{\text{guide}})\).
